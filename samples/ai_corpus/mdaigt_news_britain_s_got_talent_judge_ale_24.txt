Britain's Got Talent judge Alesha Dixon caused a stir during the show when she fell out of her dress. The 42-year-old singer and TV personality tumbled into the live audience after her dress slipped off her shoulders.  Despite the mishap, Dixon showed no signs of embarrassment as she quickly composed herself and continued with the show. Her on-stage mishap seemed to serve as a mere warm-up for a night out in London, where she made a daring fashion statement at an after-party.  Dixon opted for a backless, cutaway short dress, which showcased her toned physique and generated a lot of attention from the partygoers. The stunning outfit was a far cry from her earlier wardrobe malfunction and seemed to be a deliberate attempt to poke fun at her earlier mishap.  When asked about her daring fashion choice, Dixon seemed to take the joke in stride, saying she had learned to laugh at herself and not let mistakes get the better of her. Her ability to roll with the punches and turn a potentially embarrassing moment into a bold fashion statement has earned her a reputation as a confident and unapologetic celebrity.

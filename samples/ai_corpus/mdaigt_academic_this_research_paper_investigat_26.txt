This research paper investigates the hardness and structural properties of half-squares of restricted tree convex bipartite graphs. The objective of this study is to analyze the complexity of determining certain parameters of these graphs and explore their structural characteristics. Methodologically, we employ a combination of theoretical analysis, computational simulations, and mathematical proofs to investigate the properties of half-squares of restricted tree convex bipartite graphs. Our key findings reveal the computational hardness of certain problems related to these graphs, shedding light on their structural intricacies, particularly concerning their convexity and bipartite nature. Through our analysis, we provide insights into the unique properties and challenges associated with half-squares of restricted tree convex bipartite graphs. In conclusion, this research enhances our understanding of the complexities surrounding these graph structures and contributes to the broader field of graph theory by uncovering new insights into their hardness and structural characteristics.

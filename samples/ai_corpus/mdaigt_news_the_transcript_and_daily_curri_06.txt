The Transcript and Daily Curriculum Page Offer Assistance for Students' Reading Comprehension and News Knowledge  The Transcript and Daily Curriculum page on our website is designed to help students improve their reading skills and stay informed about current events. The Transcript section provides access to the recorded dialogue from our educational programs. This text-based version of our shows can be invaluable for students working on their reading comprehension and vocabulary development.  The Daily Curriculum, another integral part of our educational offerings, includes a Newsquiz that tests students' knowledge of recent news stories. This engaging quiz not only helps students retain the information they have learned, but also encourages them to stay up-to-date with the latest happenings in the world.  By using the Transcript in conjunction with the Daily Curriculum and Newsquiz, students can enhance their learning experience. They can read along with the speakers, carefully studying the context and meaning of each word, while also testing their understanding of the material through the Newsquiz.  Additionally, the Transcript provides an excellent resource for students working on vocabulary expansion. They can easily look up the meaning of new words they encounter during their studies, further strengthening their language skills.  We encourage students to make full use of these resources, and to provide us with their feedback.
